Scaffolding (Toolbox Talk)

This toolbox talk covers the safe erection, use and dismantling of scaffolding on site. Please read through, then ensure all attendees sign before completing.

Why It Matters

  • Scaffolding accidents are one of the top 5 causes of construction fatalities
  • HSE prosecutes incorrect erection, missing guardrails and overloading
  • A 2-minute pre-use visual check prevents 80% of incidents
  • Every team member is responsible for raising defects immediately

Key Topics

  1. Pre-use inspection & tag check (green = safe, red = do not use)
  2. PPE requirements: hard hat, hi-vis, gloves, safety boots, harness when required
  3. Loading limits — never exceed bay rating
  4. Working at height: harness, double lanyard, suitable anchor
  5. Weather conditions — wind speed cut-off 23mph, ice/snow cessation
If you are unsure about anything, ask your supervisor before proceeding. Never use a scaffold without a current green tag.

Safe Working Practices (Toolbox Talk)

General safe working practices on site.

  • Pre-task RAMS review & sign-in
  • Lifting techniques — knees not back, ask for help >25kg
  • Communication: hand signals, radios, banksman
  • Reporting near misses — every report counts

Harness and Safety Belts (Toolbox Talk)

Safe use, inspection and storage of full-body harnesses and safety belts.

  • Pre-use inspection — webbing, stitching, hardware, labels
  • Correct fit — chest strap, leg straps snug, dorsal D-ring centred
  • Anchor points — minimum 12kN rating, above shoulder height
  • Lanyard length and shock absorbers
  • Storage out of direct sunlight, away from chemicals
  • Retire any harness involved in a fall arrest immediately
Attendees: 5 signed · Given by: Dave Wilson · 04/02/2026

General Housekeeping (Toolbox Talk)

Keeping the site clean keeps the site safe.

  • Walkways clear — no trip hazards
  • Materials stored away from edges
  • Bins emptied before they overflow — fire risk
  • Sharps and offcuts disposed of correctly
  • End of shift: 10-minute clean-up before sign-out
Attendees: 5 signed · Given by: Mike Johnson · 18/02/2026

Checking Scaffold & Temporary Roofing Before Leaving Site

End-of-day inspection routine before signing out.

Daily Inspection Checklist

  1. Foundation & structural integrity — base plates seated, no settlement
  2. Platform, planking & guardrails — boards retained, gaps <25mm
  3. Access points — ladders tied, hatches closed
  4. Temporary roofing sheets — all ties & bungees secure
  5. Weather considerations — sheeting battened down for forecast wind
  6. Debris & equipment — all tools removed, area swept
If issues found: Tag red, notify supervisor, log defect before leaving site. Do not delay reporting.
Attendees: 5 signed · Given by: Tom Brady · 28/01/2026

Smoking & Vaping (Toolbox Talk)

Site smoking and vaping policy and fire-safety guidance.

  • Smoking ONLY in designated areas — see site map
  • No smoking or vaping within 10m of any LPG, fuel store or flammable material
  • Dispose of butts in metal bins only — never throw on ground
  • Vaping is treated the same as smoking on this site
  • Breach of policy = formal site warning, repeat = removal
