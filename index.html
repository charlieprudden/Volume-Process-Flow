Stage 01

Onboarding

Multiple pathways into Volume — clients choose what fits their funnel.

Onboarding options
  • Introducer sites push fully credit-checked data into Volume via API
  • Volume handles onboarding directly via API with built-in CRA integrations
  • Document generation available where needed
  • Customer criteria matching applied against agreements
Post-onboarding

Configurable due-diligence sampling across claimants and claim data. Claimants then invited to the Client Portal for documents and status updates.

02
Response & Comms
LOC + triage
Stage 02

Response & Comms

Once due diligence is complete, the Letter of Claim is submitted to the lender. Volume's triage system then processes all inbound lender correspondence.

Auto-classified types
Acknowledgement Pre-acknowledgement Already represented Defendant query Final response Customer query Investigation update

Each item is classified by the triage component and actioned with the appropriate downstream workflow.

03
Bank Details
Collect & verify
Stage 03

Bank Details Collection

A secure, standardised journey within the Client Portal — claimants are invited to log in and complete bank capture using one of two routes.

Verification routes
  • Open Banking — proves account ownership through the authorisation mechanism
  • Statement upload + CoP — extraction plus Confirmation of Payee to match name on account

Exceptions (partial or failed matches) are handled through clear evidence-submission flows. All attempts and outcomes are recorded for audit.

04
Decision & Offer
AI extraction
Stage 04

Decision & Offer

Lender decisions arrive primarily as PDF email attachments into the Volume triage system. AI integration extracts the structured fields.

Inbound channels
Direct email Shared folder Outsourcing partners Scanned PDF Text-based PDF
Calculation drivers

Workflow derives final net payable considering arrears, set-off, interest, tax on interest, law firm fees and VAT — feeding directly into the Advice Report.

Advice Report

Auto-generated using existing document generation — clearly summarises the decision, compares lender vs. firm calculation, shows transparent breakdown, and recommends accept or challenge.

05
Offer Acceptance
Client Portal
Stage 05

Offer Acceptance

Once the Advice Report is generated, the claimant receives an Email and SMS with a CTA linking to the Client Portal.

In-portal journey
  • Review the lender decision
  • Review the Advice Report
  • See the net amount receivable
  • Confirm bank details & acknowledge legal advice
  • Signature capture where lender requires
  • Accept — or query with the legal team

Configurable follow-up campaigns maximise acceptance rates. On acceptance, Volume automatically notifies the lender.

06
Payment & Settlement
Reconcile & pay
Stage 06

Payment & Settlement

Triage ingests lender remittance advice and creates expected transactions — a forward-looking view of inbound funds.

Reconciliation flow
  • PSP (incl. client account services) notifies Volume of arriving funds via API
  • Workflows reconcile against expected payments
  • Calculations re-run — actual receipt often higher due to interest
  • Volume instructs & authorises payment out to claimant and law firm
Provider

Shieldpay for initial implementation via escrow model. Underlying model is provider-agnostic and reusable across markets.

07
Closure
Audit & archive
Stage 07

Closure

Once payments are settled and reconciled, the claim moves to closure — completing the audit trail and producing the final ledger entries.

At closure
  • Final invoice raised
  • Claimant ledger updated & balanced
  • All correspondence, decisions and payments linked to the case record
  • Case archived with full auditable history

Key Actors

Claimant Submits the claim through an introducer onboarding channel.
Introducer Builds the marketing funnel to onboard claimants.
Volume The system operated by the Law Firm — case management, triage, calculations, payments.
Lender Car finance provider — receives the claim, makes a decision, sends payment.

Systems

Volume The case management platform — workflows, triage, AI extraction, calculations, reconciliation.
Client Portal Part of Volume — claimant-facing portal for documents, status updates, bank details, and offer acceptance.
Shieldpay Payment Service Provider — escrow-based payments out to claimant and law firm (provider-agnostic model).
CRA Integrations Credit Reference Agency hooks used during onboarding and document generation.
